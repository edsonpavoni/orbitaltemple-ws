INPE Logo TODO

The INPE logo needs to be manually added as inpe.webp or inpe.svg

Source: https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/f/f9/Inpelogo.png

Or download from: https://pt.wikipedia.org/wiki/Instituto_Nacional_de_Pesquisas_Espaciais

Once downloaded, convert to WebP:
ffmpeg -i inpelogo.png -q:v 85 inpe.webp -y
